Good to know

Before you begin.

Is this a horoscope?

No. PatternWise creates a personal blueprint from your birth details and focuses on recurring patterns rather than daily prediction.

Why is my exact birth time needed?

Your time and location determine the ascendant and house structure. Even a modest time difference can change the chart.

What role does AI play?

The chart calculation is deterministic. AI helps translate structured chart facts into a readable reflection; it does not calculate the astronomy.
